1. Register for a tax account number

After successful registration, you will receive an e-mail confirmation that includes your account number and PIN.

2. File and Pay the following:

School Income Tax

Wage Tax Annual Reconciliation

Business Income and Receipts EZ Return

Business Income and Receipts Tax Long Form

Net Profits Tax Return

Use & Occupancy Tax Return

Tobacco Tax Return

Pay Delinquent Tax Bill

Tax due on these returns or bill can be paid with a credit card or E-Check. A 2.49% user fee will be added to the amount due when paying by credit card or a flat fee of $1.50 when paying by E-Check up to $500,000. There will be a user fee of $3.95 for delinquent tax payments when paying by VISA Debit card. You will receive a confirmation number after filing a return. Please print a copy of your records.

This option requires a Philadelphia Tax account number and a Revenue Department Personal Identification Number (PIN). If you have forgotten your PIN or need to apply for a PIN, click here. NOTE: You cannot file and pay periodic wage tax payments online.

3. Print a Payment coupon

You must be registered for the tax type in order to print a coupon. School Income Tax and Earnings Tax coupons require a PIN number.

4. Report no tax liability for a wage tax period

If you do not have payroll for a reporting period, you must file a coupon indicating no tax due to prevent a non- filer notice.
