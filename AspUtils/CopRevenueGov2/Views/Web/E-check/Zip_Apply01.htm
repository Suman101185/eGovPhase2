Your City Account | e-check  apply

Why e-check? Convenience of paying your taxes via the Internet and no more checks and stamps. The first step is completing the application. If there is any problem with your application, you will be notified via email or you can check back to this site for your e-check status. Most applications are approved in 3 business days.

After you submit your e-check application, you are transferred to the e-check Parameter page. The e-check Parameter page is a useful tool to protect the user from mis-keying tax payment amounts.
